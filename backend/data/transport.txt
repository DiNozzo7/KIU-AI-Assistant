University (kiu) is located in Georgia, Kutaisi: ახალგაზრდობის გამზირი, მე-5 შესახვევი.

Bus number 3 travels from city center to univeristy and lastly stops at Rioni Railway Station then circles back to city center.
Bus number 25 travels from chavchavadze street (chavchavadze street is in city) to univeristy and back.

Using train as a traveling method if you want to visit different parts of georgia (rioni station) is the best, cheapest and the most comfortable way to get around around georgia.

To travel from Dorms (Dormitory) to University (or vice versa) you can use minibuses (also known as "shatli") which operate within univeristy teritory also known as "shatli". They travel in circle from dorms to university and vice versa OR students can also walk to the university. Dorms are about 1km away from university and walking takes 20-30min.
