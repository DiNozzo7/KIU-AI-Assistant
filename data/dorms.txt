Kutaisi International University Dormitory Information


Dormitory Rooms

Dormitory rooms are available only for one or two people.



Room Equipment

When moving into the dormitory, students must bring their own personal items.

The room is provided empty with only essential equipment:

- Bed
- Mattress
- Chair
- Other basic facilities



Dormitory Facilities

Dormitories include essential shared equipment such as:

- Microwaves
- Electric stoves
- Other necessary facilities



Spari Market

A Spari market is located in the F Dormitory.

Opening hours:
09:00 - 24:00



Transportation from Dormitory to University

Minibuses travel from the dormitory to the university buildings (A and K buildings).

Departure times:
:40
:45
:50
:55

Students can also walk to the university.



Laundry Facilities

Washing machines and drying machines are available on the -1 floor of the dormitory.



Study Rooms

Dormitories have dedicated study rooms available for students.



Cinema Rooms

Students have access to cinema rooms inside the dormitory.



Game Rooms

Dormitories include game rooms for entertainment and recreation.



Chess Rooms

Chess rooms are available for students.



Gym

Students have access to a gym inside the dormitory.



Health Services

Dormitory Doctor:

A doctor is available for students living in the dormitory.


Dormitory Psychology Service:

A psychology service is available for dormitory residents.
