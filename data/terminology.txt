Kutaisi International University Terminology


SA (Student Assistant)

A third or fourth-year student who conducts seminars (practical sessions).

This position is paid.



TA (Teaching Assistant)

A non-student assistant of a lecturer.

A TA has a similar role to an SA but usually has higher pay and is likely more professional.

A TA typically manages more groups than a Student Assistant.



STA (Student Teaching Assistant)

Also usually referred to as a Student Tutor.

A student who has already completed a course and helps other students successfully complete it.

Becoming an STA is one of the ways to earn an ECAS point.



CE (Central Exercise)

The practical part of a lecture where exercises are discussed.

Usually conducted by the lecturer, or by a TA if the lecturer does not conduct it.



TTF (Test-Tutor Feedback)

A period where students can ask questions from SAs or TAs.

It is a practical session conducted in smaller groups (up to 25 students) instead of the entire course.

TTF usually lasts one hour.

There may also be a 20-30 minute test depending on the course.



Lab (Laboratory)

A practical session similar to TTF but mainly used for programming courses.

The standard duration was 2 hours.
