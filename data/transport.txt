Kutaisi International University Transportation Information

Bus Routes

Bus No. 3 travel from the city center to the university and back.
Bus No. 25 travels from chavchavadze street to university and back

Rioni Railway Station Stop

The bus No. 3 also stops at Rioni Railway Station.

Earliest Departure

The first bus leaves at: 7:50

Using train (rioni station) is the best, cheapest and the most comfortable way to get around around georgia.

Important Note

Estimated bus times do not account for major delays or early arrivals.

Transportation from Dormitory - University

Minibuses travel in circle, from dorms to university and vice versa.

Departure times: :40-:00

Students can also walk to the university. Dorms are about 1km away from university and walking takes 20-30min.
